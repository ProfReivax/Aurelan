#People 

- Nickname: "Spice"

- **Species:** Thérionide (Catlike features — short tawny fur, bright golden eyes, and tufted ears that flick when she’s amused)

- **Description:** A lively middle-aged Thérionide woman who runs her open-air kitchen with effortless grace. Her laugh rings through Kael’s Rest as she stirs bubbling pots and flips flatbreads with her tail. Known for her knack at blending exotic flavors and for her ability to spot a hungry traveler from across the square.

- **Personality:** Chatty, warm, and endlessly curious. She has a teasing humor and a maternal streak for the market’s younger vendors.
