#People 

Once a perfumer and alchemist, now the most influential dealer in pleasure, addiction, and escape. Her parties are legendary, her enemies vanished in “accidents.”  

**Personality:** Charismatic, indulgent, and serpentine. She speaks softly and smiles as others destroy themselves.  

**Goal:** Ensure Marcherion’s citizens never stop craving what only she can provide — ecstasy, oblivion, or both.
