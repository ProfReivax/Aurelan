#Organisation 

### 1. **[[The Iron Fangs]]**

- **Leader:** _[[Commander Varek Thorne]]_ Also the City Marshal of [[Marcherion]]
- **Type:** Elite Mercenary Company   
- **Specialty:** Shock troops and bodyguards, famed for brutal efficiency in close combat.  
- **Influence:** Holds many of the city's personal protection contracts, including for Concord members. They also serve as the **primary force of the city’s guards and standing army**, maintaining order and enforcing the council’s will through muscle and discipline.

### 2. **[[The Silver Scales]]**

- **Leader:** _[[Lyrien Daelcor]]_
- **Type:** Merchant Guild 
- **Specialty:** Trade in precious metals, rare minerals, and crafted weapons. Controls the flow of luxury goods through the city.  
- **Influence:** Masters of tariff collection and black-market dealings.
### 3. **[[The Veiled Blades]]**

- **Leader:** _[[Mistress Selka Varn]]_
- **Type:** Assassin’s Guild / Espionage Network   
- **Specialty:** Covert operations, intelligence gathering, and “discreet” problem solving.    
- **Influence:** Keeps many Concord members’ secrets — and enforces their blackmail.

### 4. **[[The Crimson Wolves]]**

- **Leader:** _[[Captain Dorran Halvyr]]_
- **Type:** Mercenary Company  
- **Specialty:** Guerrilla warfare, ambush tactics, and wilderness survival. Often hired for border skirmishes and raids.    
- **Influence:** Holds sway over the city’s more militant frontier interests.

### 5. **[[The Ember Pact]]**

- **Leader:** _[[Magister Kira Volanth]]_
- **Type:** Mercenary Company / Pyromancers and Demolition Experts    
- **Specialty:** Use alchemical fire, explosives, and flame magic to devastate battlefields.    
- **Influence:** Feared for their scorched-earth tactics. The Blackened Anvils rely on them for testing new weaponry, and the Iron Concord employs them when subtlety fails.

### 6. **[[The Iron Scribes]]**

- **Leader:** _[[Archivist Jethren Moll]]_
- **Type:** Administrative Guild    
- **Specialty:** Contract drafting, magical sealing of agreements, and legal arbitration.    
- **Influence:** Their approval is necessary for any official document or contract in the city.

### 7. **[[The Blackened Anvils]]**

- **Leader:** _[[Forge-Master Helra Durn]]_
- **Type:** Artisan Guild
- **Specialty:** Blacksmithing, armorer crafting, and siege weapon manufacture.
- **Influence:** Supplies the bulk of the city’s arms and armor, and controls prices through monopolies.

### 8. **[[The Gilded Ledger]]**

- **Leader:** _[[Magnate Othran Vale]]_
- **Type:** Banking and Finance Guild    
- **Specialty:** Traditional bankers, financiers, and moneylenders who handle loans, investments, and currency exchange.    
- **Influence:** Controls the city’s official financial institutions and vaults. They grant or withhold credit that can make or break mercenary companies.

### 9. **[[The Black Chalice]]**

- **Leader:** _[[Madame Serah Vell]]_
- **Type:** Brewers, Distillers, and Alchemists of Vice  
- **Specialty:** Produces and distributes the city’s most sought-after alcohols, narcotics, and mood-enhancing elixirs — from fine wines to hallucinogenic draughts.    
- **Influence:** Controls the flow of pleasure in Marcherion. They sponsor festivals, taverns, and private dens. Officially a “guild of artisans,” unofficially, they own the city’s nights.

### 10. **[[The Obsidian Chorus]]**

- **Leader:** _[[Archmage Talan Quorr]]_
- **Type:** Arcane Guild   
- **Specialty:** Magical research, enchantments, and battlefield support spells.   
- **Influence:** Provides magical services and enforces the city’s arcane laws.

### 11. **[[The Steel Jaws]]**

- **Leader:** _[[Marshal Bren Irondrake]]_
- **Type:** Mercenary Company / Heavy Weapons and Engineers
- **Specialty:** Masters of heavy weaponry, siege engines, and battlefield engineering.
- **Influence:** They maintain the city’s arsenal of siege engines and war machines, making them crucial to both offense and defense.

### 12. **[[The Iron Ravens]]**

- **Leader:** _[[Master Corven Draal]]_
- **Type:** Courier and Intelligence Guild
- **Specialty:** Message delivery, spying, and surveillance throughout the city and beyond.
- **Influence:** Acts as the city’s unseen eyes and ears, manipulating information flow.


### 13. **[[The Twelve Pillars]]**

- **Leader:** _[[High Seeker Malen Tareth]]_
- **Type:** Religious Guild
- **Specialty:** Guardians and administrators of the city’s temples and places of worship. They ensure religious practices continue amid the mercenary chaos and serve as mediators between faith communities and the Iron Concord.  
- **Influence:** Control temple construction, maintenance, and clergy appointments. They maintain the delicate balance where religion is respected as one of the few genuine sources of community and respite in Marcherion. The Twelve Pillars also arbitrate disputes involving religious law and oversee sacred festivals, reinforcing the city’s cultural cohesion.
