#Religion 
## **Basic Description:**

The god of Nature embodies the raw forces and rhythms that govern the natural world beyond mortal control. Neither benevolent guardian nor cruel destroyer, this deity represents the wildness, balance, and eternal cycles that shape the earth, sky, and seas. Followers see Nature as the great equalizer, indifferent to human notions of good and evil, embodying both calm and tempest, growth and dormancy, order and chaos.
