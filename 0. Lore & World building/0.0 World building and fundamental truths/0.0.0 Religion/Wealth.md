#Religion 
## **Basic Description:**

The god of Wealth governs prosperity, abundance, and the flow of resources that sustain life and power. Neither a mere giver nor hoarder of riches, this deity embodies the balance between gain and loss, generosity and greed. To some followers, the god is a benevolent patron of fortune and growth; to others, a stern judge of how wealth is earned and spent.
