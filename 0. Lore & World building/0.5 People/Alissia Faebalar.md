Alissia Faebalar is a 236-year-old elven professor of arcane law, magical ethics, and artificing, renowned for her deep knowledge of both spellcraft and magical inventions. Patient and incisive, she guides young mages and artificers alike through the complexities of magical regulation, helping them understand their rights and responsibilities. Her love for crafting and studying magic items fuels her teaching, making her a sought-after mentor for those who wish to master both theory and practical creation.
