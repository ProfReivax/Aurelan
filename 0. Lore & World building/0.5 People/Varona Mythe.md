#People 
Varona Mythe is a 147-year-old elven professor of arcane law, magical ethics, and artificing, renowned for her deep knowledge of both spellcraft and magical inventions. Patient and incisive, she guides young mages and artificers alike through the complexities of magical regulation, helping them understand their rights and responsibilities. Her love for crafting and studying magic items fuels her teaching, making her a sought-after mentor for those who wish to master both theory and practical creation. Varona is often the first person aspiring spellcasters and inventors turn to when facing injustice related to their magic or creations, offering clear guidance and steadfast support.
