	#religion

**Basic Description:**  
The god of knowledge embodies both the pursuit of wisdom and the responsibility that comes with it. For the clerics who follow the enlightened path, the focus is on sharing knowledge, fostering wisdom, and illuminating the world with truth.


**Tenets of the Enlightened Path:**

1. **Pursue Wisdom:** Always seek to learn and grow, for knowledge is a neve r-ending journey.
    
2. **Share Together:** Enlightenment flourishes when wisdom is shared. Offer your knowledge .
    
3. **Uplift Others:** Use knowledge to empower, to heal, and to guide others toward enlightenment.
    
4. **Embrace Responsibility:** With great wisdom comes the duty to protect and preserve it, never letting power corrupt your purpose.

**Verses of Enlightenment:**

- _"In the light of knowledge, shadows fade, and the soul finds its way."_
    
- _"To teach is to kindle a flame that burns eternally, illuminating the world."_
    
- _"In every question lies a path; in every answer, a new dawn."_
